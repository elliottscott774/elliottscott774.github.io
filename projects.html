Elliott Scott's Data Science Projects

  • Soccer Transfer Recommendation System

    A project aimed at aiding soccer clubs in finding potential transfer targets through a mix of statistics and machine learning. It features a recommendation system that evaluates player statistics and team needs, developed using Python, Pandas, NumPy, and Streamlit, with data storage on Backblaze B2.

  • Statistical Analysis of NFL Success

    An in-depth statistical analysis project exploring factors contributing to NFL teams' success. Utilizes logistic regression models to evaluate performance metrics beyond winning the Super Bowl, aiming to provide insights into team strengths and areas for improvement.

    View Analysis | View Project Slides

  • Exploratory Analysis of Manchester United vs Tottenham Hotspur

    On August 19, 2023, Tottenham Hotspur outplayed Manchester United with a 2-0 victory. This project delves into the match's dynamics through data analysis and visualization. The analysis covers shot-creating actions, expected goals, passing strategies, and defensive tactics, highlighting Manchester United's missed opportunities and areas for improvement.

    View Analysis

  • Baseball Simulation for the Children's Museum of Indianapolis

    This project was created for an event at The Children's Museum of Indianapolis, aiming to teach children about the power of analytics in understanding sports. It features a Python-based simulation of baseball games, where players' performances are generated based on statistical probabilities, and the goal is predict the best batter. The project helps in illustrating the impact of player statistics on understanding the outcome, making it a fun and educational tool for introducing children to data science and analytics.
